Licensing

Licensing overview

The DollhouseMCP software project is open source under AGPL-3.0, with commercial licensing available for teams that need different terms.

Software license

DollhouseMCP software is released under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0. That keeps the core platform open while allowing users to study, modify, run, and share the software under AGPL terms.

DollhouseMCP software AGPL-3.0 Applies to the core open-source product and source releases.
Commercial licensing Available by request For organizations that need alternative terms for proprietary or hosted use cases.
This website repository Separate repository terms Website content and code may be governed separately from the core product license.

When commercial licensing may help

  • You want to integrate DollhouseMCP into proprietary systems without AGPL obligations.
  • You need custom terms for hosted offerings, support, or distribution.
  • You want a direct licensing path for enterprise procurement or legal review.

Questions and contact

For software licensing questions or commercial inquiries, contact contact@dollhousemcp.com.
